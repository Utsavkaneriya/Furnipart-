We Believe You Are UniqueWine is an alcoholic drink made from fermented fruit. Yeast consumes the sugar in the fruit and converts it to ethanol and carbon dioxide, releasing heat in the process. Wine is most often made from grapes, and the term "wine" generally refers to grape wine when used without any qualification.

Wine is any alcoholic beverage made from fermented fruit juice, but the term is most often used to describe beverages made from fermented grapes. The natural chemical makeup of grapes allows fermentation to occur without adding extra sugar, acid, or other ingredients.
